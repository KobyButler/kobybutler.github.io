Questions

Common Questions

Do I have to pay every month to keep the site up?
No. You pay once for me to build it. The only ongoing cost is domain registration (your website address, like "yourbusiness.com") which is typically around $10–15 per year — and that goes directly to the domain provider, not to me.
What if I need changes after it's done?
One round of revisions is included before launch. After that, I'm happy to help with small updates — just reach out and we'll figure out a fair arrangement. For most simple changes, it won't cost much at all.
I'm not tech-savvy at all. Is that okay?
Absolutely. You don't need to know anything about websites. I handle everything technical. All I need from you is information about your business and whatever photos or content you want on the site.
How long does it take?
Typically 2–3 weeks from when we start. The faster you can get me your content (photos, text, etc.), the faster we move.
What kinds of businesses do you work with?
Any local business that needs a web presence — restaurants, service providers, guides, shops, contractors, you name it. If you have customers, you need a website.
